Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the sugars and butter.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla.
  4. Add baking soda, salt, flour, and chips.
  5. Drop onto baking sheets.
  6. Bake 10 mins or until edges brown.

Why Cookies Are Good

Chocolate chip cookies are a classic treat, loved for their soft centers, crispy edges, and melty chocolate. Cookies are good because they are delicious, comforting, and easy to enjoy. Their sweet flavor, soft or crunchy texture, and endless variety make them a favorite treat for people of all ages. Cookies are also convenient to eat on the go, simple to bake, and fun to share with friends or family. Many people connect cookies with happy memories, like baking at home or enjoying them during holidays, which adds emotional warmth to every bite. Whether homemade or store-bought, cookies bring joy and satisfaction in a small, tasty package. Be honest, cookies are often delicious! The taste, texture, and aroma can provide a moment of simple pleasure and satisfaction.
